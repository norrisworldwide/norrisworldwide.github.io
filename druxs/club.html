Our club is for all activities surrounding the bike (except the cafes). The services, which we offer in the store are for club members only. But do not worry if you want to use our services, you can request a service member during opening times. The price is €2.40 and is valid for one calendar year. After one year the membership has to be renewed, otherwise it expires automatically.

The service membership offers many possibilities, such as round tables, exchange, etc. A full membership, costs €48 per year and mainly includes joint training sessions, club tours and a place in our team. After you complete your license you can be an official hit!

We accept indoor cyclists and cyclists of all classes, street riders and triathletes.

Club members must participate in training in club clothes. Druxs team clothes can be purchased at the shop.

For more information regarding membership, please contact us.
