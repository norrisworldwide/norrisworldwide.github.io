Welcome to our website! We are pleased that you have found the virtual way to us!

We are a small family in Munich and have existed since 2011. Our passion is racing, primarily steel road bikes. Many years of experience as an athlete on the road bike and several years as a professional racing cyclist means we know what really matters.

Over time, a deep attachment and love has arisen for steel road bikes. This love is here at Druxs again.

All racing bikes that we offer are characterized by high quality and a stylish design. We restore vintage road bikes (with frame and components from the late 80's and older) and fabricate to your individual, tailored needs and desires.

Our house brand "Druxs Road", is in great demand.

We buy and sell quality steel racing bikes, bike parts and accessories - from classic to modern. Here you will also find Druxs tested track and route bikes.

We will advise you in all matters concerning the road.
