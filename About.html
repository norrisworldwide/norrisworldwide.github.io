Mitch Norris is an Australian who has lived and shot photos all around the world. Infact, he has taken photos on every continent except Antartica.
He currently presides in Munich, Germany with his wife.

He draws inspiration from the ever change urban and natural landscape.
His style is a blend of by documentary, street, travel, portraiture and landscape photography, without specialising in any area in particular.
He captures moments which condure up feelings of beauty, despair, awe, sadness, love, anger, elation and much more in those that view his work.

Almost all of his photos are taken on 35mm film and analog cameras, ranging from the
Leica M6, Olympus 35DC, Nikon F501, Olympus 35RC, Olympus XA and many more.
But his current go to cameras are his two Olympus Mju-ii, his Minolta CLE and his Olympus OM2n.
